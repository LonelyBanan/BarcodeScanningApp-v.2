# Installation Guide - Predmet Furniture Warehouse Inventory

This app is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that can be installed on both PC and Android devices for offline use.

## Features
- ✅ Dark/Light theme toggle
- ✅ Works offline after installation
- ✅ Installable on PC (Windows, Mac, Linux)
- ✅ Installable on Android phones
- ✅ Barcode/QR scanning with camera
- ✅ Google Sheets integration
- ✅ GPS location tracking

---

## Installation on PC

### Chrome/Edge (Windows, Mac, Linux)
1. Open the app URL in Chrome or Edge browser
2. Look for the **install icon** (⊕) in the address bar (right side)
3. Click the install icon
4. Click "Install" in the popup dialog
5. The app will be installed and open in its own window
6. You can now access it from:
   - **Windows**: Start Menu or Desktop shortcut
   - **Mac**: Applications folder or Launchpad
   - **Linux**: Applications menu

**Alternative method:**
1. Click the **3 dots menu** (⋮) in the top right
2. Select "Install Predmet Furniture Warehouse Inventory..."
3. Click "Install"

### Firefox (Windows, Mac, Linux)
1. Open the app URL in Firefox
2. Click the **3 lines menu** (≡) in the top right
3. Select "Install Predmet Furniture Warehouse Inventory..."
4. Click "Install"

---

## Installation on Android

### Chrome on Android
1. Open the app URL in Chrome
2. Tap the **3 dots menu** (⋮) in the top right
3. Select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap "Install" in the popup
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen
6. Tap the icon to open the app

**Banner prompt:**
- If you see a banner at the bottom saying "Install App", simply tap "Install"

### Samsung Internet
1. Open the app URL in Samsung Internet
2. Tap the **menu icon** (3 lines) at the bottom
3. Select "Add page to" → "Home screen"
4. The app will be added to your home screen

## Using the App

### Theme Toggle
- Click the **sun/moon icon** in the top right corner
- Choose between:
  - **Light** - Light theme
  - **Dark** - Dark theme
  - **System** - Follow your device's theme

### Camera Access (for scanning)
When you first use the scanner:
1. The app will ask for camera permission
2. Tap/Click "Allow"
3. Your camera will activate for barcode/QR scanning

### GPS Location (for search tracking)
When you search for an item:
1. The app will ask for location permission
2. Tap/Click "Allow"
3. Your GPS coordinates will be saved with the search

### Google Sheets Sync
1. Make your Google Sheet publicly accessible:
   - Open Google Sheet → Share → "Anyone with the link can view"
2. Copy the share URL
3. In the app, click "Sync from Google Sheets"
4. Paste the URL and click "Sync Now"

## Uninstalling

### PC
- **Windows**: Right-click the app icon → Uninstall
- **Mac**: Drag from Applications to Trash
- **Chrome/Edge**: Settings → Apps → Find the app → Uninstall

### Android
1. Long-press the app icon
2. Tap "App info" or drag to "Uninstall"
3. Confirm uninstall

## Troubleshooting

### App won't install
- Ensure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)
- Check you have sufficient storage space
- Try clearing browser cache

### Camera not working
- Check browser/app has camera permission
- Ensure no other app is using the camera
- Try reloading the page

### GPS not working
- Enable location services on your device
- Grant location permission to the browser/app
- Ensure you're not in airplane mode
