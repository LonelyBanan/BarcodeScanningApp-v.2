# Quick Install Guide

## 📱 Install on Android

1. Open the app URL in **Chrome**
2. Tap **"Install App"** banner at the bottom
   - OR tap ⋮ menu → "Install app"
3. Tap **"Install"**
4. Done! App icon appears on home screen

## 💻 Install on PC

### Windows/Mac/Linux

1. Open the app URL in **Chrome** or **Edge**
2. Click the **install icon** (⊕) in address bar
3. Click **"Install"**
4. Done! App opens in its own window

**Or:** Click ⋮ menu → "Install..."

---

## 🎨 Switch Theme

Click the **sun/moon icon** in top right corner:
- ☀️ Light
- 🌙 Dark
- 🖥️ System (auto)

---

## 📷 Enable Camera (for scanning)

First scan:
1. Click "Scan" tab
2. Click **"Allow"** when prompted
3. Camera activates

---

## 📍 Enable GPS (for search tracking)

First search:
1. Click "Search" tab
2. Enter item name/code
3. Click **"Allow"** when prompted
4. Location saved

---

## 📊 Sync Google Sheets

1. Share your Google Sheet: "Anyone with link can view"
2. Copy the share URL
3. In app: Click **"Sync from Google Sheets"**
4. Paste URL → **"Sync Now"**

---

## ✅ You're All Set!

The app now works:
- ✅ Offline
- ✅ With camera
- ✅ With GPS tracking
- ✅ Syncs with Google Sheets

Enjoy! 🚀
